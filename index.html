1. Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The App:

All data created within the App (including notes or stopwatch records) is stored locally on the user's device only.

No data is transmitted to external servers.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate third-party analytics, advertising, tracking tools, or external data processing services.

4. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children.

5. User Rights (GDPR)

Since the App does not collect or process personal data, there is no personal data stored, accessed, modified, or deleted by the developer.
